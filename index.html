Donation

Donations during Ramadan are usually made towards the end of the holy month of Ramadan. These donations include Zakat, al-Fitr, and Charity. While there is no compulsion as to giving donations during Ramadan except al-Fitr, it is suggested that any act of charity during Ramadan brings one closer to Allah and gain a deeper understanding of human suffering.
